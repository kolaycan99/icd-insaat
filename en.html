About Us

Engineering and Consulting Center

ICD İnşaat is not just a material supplier or application company. We are an engineering and consulting center acting on the principle of accurate diagnosis first, then the right solution in the construction sector.

Founded with over 20 years of industry experience and a civil engineering vision, our company specializes in the most critical parts of structures: industrial flooring systems, exterior facade solutions, and waterproofing.

Our Process

Engineering Management in 3 Basic Stages

Unlike many companies in the sector, we manage our work in 3 basic stages.

01

Technical Consulting & Specifications SPEC-In

The most important stage of a project is accurate planning. We perform technical analyses during the design stage, determine the most suitable systems for the structure, and prepare engineering specifications.

02

Strategic Material Supply
